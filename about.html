About Me

Hello! I'm Jayesh Shukla, a passionate UI/UX Designer and Developer from Pratapgarh, India. I specialize in creating visually appealing and highly functional websites with a focus on responsive web design.

My journey began with a Diploma in Computer Science and Engineering, and I am currently pursuing my B.Sc. in Computer Science and Engineering as well. I have always been fascinated by the blend of creativity and technical skills required in web development, and this led me to dive deep into the world of UI/UX design and development.

I have hands-on experience in designing responsive websites that adapt seamlessly across various devices, ensuring users have the best experience no matter where they access the site from. I aim to combine aesthetics with functionality to provide an optimized digital experience.

I am continuously learning and growing my skills in frontend development and UI/UX design to stay up-to-date with industry trends. I am passionate about creating user-centric, intuitive designs and improving my coding practices to build faster, more responsive websites.
